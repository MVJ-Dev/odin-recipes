Gnocchi al pesto

Gnocchis al pesto is a delicious Italian dish that combines soft, pillowy gnocchi with vibrant pesto sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of potatoes
  • All-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Pine nuts
  • Parmesean cheese
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • lemon juice

Steps

For the gnocchis

  1. Boil potatoes until tender. Leet cool, then peel.
  2. Mash potatoes until smooth.
  3. ombine mashed potatoes, flour, egg (if using), and salt. Form a soft dough
  4. Roll dough into logs and cut into small pieces. Use a fork to create ridges (optional).
  5. Cook gnocchi in salted boiling water until they float (about 2-3 minutes). Remove and drain.

    6. For the pesto

  6. In a food processor, combine basil, pine nuts, Parmesan, garlic, and salt. Blend while slowly adding olive oil until smooth.
  7. Taste and adjust seasoning or add lemon juice if desired.
  8. Then mix the cooked gnocchis with the pesto until well coated.
